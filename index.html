Food Insecurity In America

Food Insecurity is a growing concern that limits access to healthy, sufficient and affordable food.
These inadequacies are impacting people of all ages within low income families and communities as a whole.
Many are suffering from diseases and setbacks because of the lack of proper food nutrition and accessbility.
